## Content Ideas for an EOC website

> Content Ideas for an EOC website

## Possible Sitemap

### Public Pages

- Home
  - Welcome
  - Who we are
  - Navigation
- Temporal Information (i.e., blog)
- "Timeless" information
  - Hazards
  - How to Prepare

### Internal (Private) Pages

- Sign-in (who has access, to all or part of the site?)
- Current Incidents
  - Incidents
    - For Each Incident
      - IAP
        - Objectives
          - Strategies
        - Staffing
      - Situation Awareness
      - Missions
  - Contact Info
    - EOC Sections
      - Units
    - Volunteers
      - CERT
      - ACS
      - MRC
    - Partners
      - Vendors
      - Regional Partners
      - State or County Agencies/Departments
    - Wiki
      - Alerting
      - User Guides
    - Std Op Proceedures
    - Planning
    -
